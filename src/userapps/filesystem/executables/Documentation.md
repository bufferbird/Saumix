# Documentation ShellBasic

ShellBasic (formerly ShellBasicScript) is a hybrid out of Bash, Unix and a former other language from me,
wich gives shb a Syntax with the : and goto logic of Bash, the - flags of Unix and the ! Syntax of my
Messenger Admin Script. You can find Examples of Script's at src/userapps/filesystem/executables/includables/shb/examples. 

## Includable

Probably you were thinking, what /includables/ means. That shows the feature, that it is planned, that .shb and the not as dangerous and safer version .shb1 
Script's can be written, and system and App Files can go to them, like dll's in windows, but you can also write own apps wth it, without having to do complicated 
apk things etc. 

## Differents between .shb1 and .shb

The Different between the both types of ShellBasic is, that .shb can get to iternals, and change some of them, but to get security, 
to execute .shb you have to Click a Window, or switch it to an other place, this does not exist in .shb1, because it cant write in System Internals. 

### Hardware Communication

.shb and, but not as deep, .shb1 allow Hardware activating or to change their Status, and If master or slave and so on (only .shb)
